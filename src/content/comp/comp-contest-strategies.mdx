### Contest Strategies Overview

Competitive programming contests require not just problem-solving skills, but also strategy and time management.

### Key Strategies

- Read all problems first
- Solve easy problems quickly
- Don't get stuck too long
- Manage your time
- Debug efficiently
- Practice under timed conditions

### After the Contest

- Upsolve missed problems
- Analyze mistakes
- Review editorials
- Track your progress
